Media Coverage on COVID-19 in Hong Kong

Limited supply of masks

It is proved that wearing a high-quality of mask, helps protect people by reducing the chance of spreading COVID-19. In the beginning of the pandemic, the supply of masks can not fulfill its demand. The selling price of mask mutiplied. People had to queued up for a box of mask in the earliy morning. In addition, the Hong Kong government launched the "Local Mask Production Subsidy Scheme" to solve the shortage of masks.

The controversy over the isolation camp

  1. Chun Yeung Village, an unoccupied public housing, treated as a quarantine center without consultion. The arrangement caused dissatisfaction among admitted tenants of Chun Yeung Estate and residents of Fo Tan. District councilors and civilians have held numerous rallies, marches and petitions to oppose the government's use of the place as a quarantine center.



  2. Tsing Yi Makeshift Hospital, an isolation facility in Tsing Yi. Many people have complained about the problems of the poor meal and living condition. The TVB program "Scoop" interviewed the people and filmed the living era of Tsing Yi Fangcai Hospital. News

Public Examination Arrangement

Under the pandemic, students are not allowed to have face-to-face lessons. In March 2022, the number of infected cases increased rapidly. Peopleare suggested to stay at home. Therefore the starting date of the Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education Examination could not be confirmed. Until the early April, Secretary for Education Kevin Yeung Yun-hung announced that the exam would be held on 22 April. News
